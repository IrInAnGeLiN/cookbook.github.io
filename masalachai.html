Masala Chai (India)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 black tea bags
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 2 tsp sugar

Introduction

A spiced tea with warming flavors of ginger, cinnamon, and cardamom.

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring water, tea, and spices to a boil.
  2. Add milk and sugar, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Strain and serve hot.

Cultural Background

Masala Chai is a spiced tea beverage that originated in India. It's a daily ritual for many across the Indian subcontinent.

Cooking Tip

Crush the spices slightly before adding to release more flavour.

Substitution

Use non-dairy milk for a vegan version.
