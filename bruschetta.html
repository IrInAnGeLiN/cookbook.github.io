Bruschetta (Italy)

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 3 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Introduction

A classic Italian appetizer featuring toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil. Simple, yet bursting with flavor!

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5-7 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spoon the mixture onto toasted bread. Serve immediately.

Cultural Background

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer, often served as a starter before pasta or meat dishes.

Cooking Tip

For extra flavor, rub the toasted bread with a clove of garlic before adding the toppings.

Substitution

Use gluten-free bread for a gluten-free version.
