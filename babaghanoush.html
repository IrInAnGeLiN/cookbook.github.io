Baba Ghanoush (Middle East)

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste

Introduction

Fresh seafood marinated in lime juice with chili, onions, and cilantro, creating a tangy and refreshing dish.

Instructions

  1. Roast the eggplants over an open flame or in an oven at 220°C (430°F) until soft.
  2. Peel the skin off the roasted eggplants and discard.
  3. Mash the eggplants in a bowl until smooth.
  4. Add tahini, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, and salt to the mashed eggplants.
  5. Mix all ingredients thoroughly until well combined.
  6. Serve with pita bread or fresh vegetables.

Cultural Background

A staple in Middle Eastern cuisine, baba ghanoush is often served as part of a mezze platter.

Cooking Tip

Charring the eggplant over an open flame adds a deeper smoky flavor.

Substitution

Use Greek yogurt for a creamier texture.
