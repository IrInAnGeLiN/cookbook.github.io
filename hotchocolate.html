Mexican Hot Chocolate (Mexico)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 100g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp sugar

Introduction

A rich and spiced version of hot chocolate made with cinnamon and a touch of chili.

Instructions

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan until warm.
  2. Add chocolate, cinnamon, chili, and sugar. Stir until melted.
  3. Serve hot with whipped cream if desired.

Cultural Background

A spiced chocolate drink from Mexico, with ancient roots in Aztec and Mayan cultures.

Cooking Tip

Add cayenne pepper or smoked paprika for a spicier kick.

Substitution

Use almond or oat milk for a dairy-free version.
