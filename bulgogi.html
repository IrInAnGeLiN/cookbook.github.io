Bulgogi (Korea)

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (ribeye or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Introduction

A sweet and savory marinated grilled beef dish, a Korean BBQ favorite.

Instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and sesame seeds.
  2. Marinate beef for at least 1 hour.
  3. Grill or pan-fry until caramelized.
  4. Serve with rice and kimchi.

Cultural Background

Bulgogi is a staple of Korean cuisine, enjoyed at family gatherings and barbecues.

Cooking Tip

For extra tenderness, add a grated pear to the marinade.

Substitution

Use chicken or tofu for a variation.
