Paella (Spain)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups arborio or paella rice
  • 1/2 kg shrimp
  • 1/2 kg mussels
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp saffron
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Introduction

A rich and colourful Spanish rice dish cooked with seafood, saffron, and vegetables.

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Saut√© garlic and bell pepper.
  2. Add rice, saffron, paprika, and mix well.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add shrimp and mussels, cover, and cook until seafood is done.
  5. Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Cultural Background

Paella originates from Valencia, Spain, and is a festive dish enjoyed at gatherings.

Cooking Tip

Use a wide, shallow pan to achieve the signature crispy rice layer at the bottom.

Substitution

Replace seafood with chicken and chorizo for a meatier version.
