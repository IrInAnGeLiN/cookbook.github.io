Baklava (Turkey)

Ingredients

  • 1 pack filo pastry
  • 2 cups chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup honey

Introduction

A rich, sweet dessert made of layers of filo pastry, chopped nuts, and honey syrup.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Brush a baking dish with butter. Layer filo sheets, brushing each with butter.
  3. Spread nuts, sugar, and cinnamon over the layers.
  4. Add more filo layers, brushing with butter.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Drizzle with honey and let cool.

Cultural Background

Baklava is a popular dessert across the Middle East and Mediterranean regions.

Cooking Tip

Cut into pieces before baking for easier serving.

Substitution

Use maple syrup instead of honey for a different flavor.
