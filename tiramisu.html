Tiramisu (Italy)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed espresso
  • 200g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Introduction

A famous Italian dessert made of layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix mascarpone, sugar, heavy cream, and vanilla.
  2. Dip ladyfingers into espresso and layer them in a dish.
  3. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat the layers, finishing with mascarpone on top.
  5. Dust with cocoa powder and refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.

Cultural Background

Tiramisu means "pick me up" in Italian, referring to its coffee content.

Cooking Tip

Use freshly brewed espresso for the best flavor.

Substitution

Use decaf coffee for a caffeine-free version.
