Ceviche (Peru)

Ingredients

  • 500g white fish (sea bass, tilapia), diced
  • 1 cup lime juice
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Introduction

Fresh seafood marinated in lime juice with chili, onions, and cilantro, creating a tangy and refreshing dish.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine fish and lime juice. Let marinate for 20-30 minutes until the fish turns opaque.
  2. Add onions, chili, cilantro, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Serve chilled, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Cultural Background

Ceviche is a national dish of Peru, with variations found throughout Latin America.

Cooking Tip

Use the freshest fish possible, as it is "cooked" only by the acidity of lime juice.

Substitution

For a milder version, remove the seeds from the chili.
