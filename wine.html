Glühwein (Germany)

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle red wine
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 5 cloves
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 star anise

Introduction

A warm, spiced mulled wine perfect for winter nights.

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine all ingredients.
  2. Heat gently without boiling for 20 minutes.
  3. Serve warm in mugs.

Cultural Background

Glühwein is a traditional German drink served at Christmas markets.

Cooking Tip

Do not let it boil, as it will lose alcohol and flavour.

Substitution

Use grape juice for a non-alcoholic version.
