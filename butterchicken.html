Butter Chicken (India)

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Introduction

A creamy and flavourful Indian dish with tender chicken cooked in a rich tomato sauce.

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add onions and garlic, sauté until soft.
  2. Add chicken, cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato purée, cream, and spices. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice or naan bread.

Cultural Background

Originating from North India, butter chicken is now a global favorite.

Cooking Tip

Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices overnight for more flavor.

Substitution

Use coconut milk instead of cream for a dairy-free version.
